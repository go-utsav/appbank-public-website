Frequently Asked Questions

How Can I Reset My Online Banking Password?

You Can Reset Your Password By Clicking On The 'Forgot Password' Link On The Login Page And Following The Instructions.

What Should I Do If I Lose My Debit Card?

If you lose your debit card, immediately lock your card through the app or call our 24/7 customer service. We'll help you block the card and issue a replacement.

How Do I Set Up Direct Deposit?

Setting up direct deposit is easy. Simply provide your employer with your account number and routing number, which can be found in your app under Account Details.

Are My Online Banking Transactions Secure?

Yes, we use industry-leading encryption and security measures to protect all your transactions. We also provide two-factor authentication for additional security.

Can I Transfer Money To Another Bank?

Yes, you can easily transfer money to other banks through our app using ACH transfer, wire transfer, or instant transfer options.
